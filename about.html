Greetings!
My name is Lena and I am a professional artist. I came to Canada from Ukraine in 2022 because of the war. In each painting I want to convey my personal vision of the beauty and uniqueness of everything that exists.

My vision
I like to make copies of paintings by great realist artists. It is always a useful practice. For me there are no insignificant paintings or drawings, each sketch reflects a unique moment of life. Although painting is not my main occupation, that for me is primarily a spiritual activity.

My experience
I like to share my skills and I give workshops and studios for everyone who wants to immerse themselves in the magical world of painting. Also sometimes I exhibit my paintings at The Barrie Art Club.
